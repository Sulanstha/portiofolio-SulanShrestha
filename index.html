Boudha, or Boudhanath, is an important spiritual hub in Kathmandu, Nepal, famous for its massive and ancient stupa, a UNESCO World Heritage Site. It is a primary center of Tibetan Buddhism in Nepal and one of the largest spherical stupas in the world

The Pashupatinath Temple is a sacred Hindu temple complex located on the banks of the Bagmati River in Kathmandu, Nepal. It is one of the four most important religious sites in Asia for devotees of Lord Shiva and a UNESCO World Heritage Site since 1979
